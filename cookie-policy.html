What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help sites remember your preferences and understand how visitors use the site.

Cookies we use

We use very few cookies. Here is the complete list:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
pnu_cookies_accepted Functional Remembers whether you accepted or declined cookies on this site. Set by us, not shared with anyone. Persistent (localStorage)
_fbp Analytics / Advertising Set by the Meta Pixel (ID: 517991158551582) to measure ad performance and understand how visitors find us. Only set if you click "Accept" on the cookie banner. 90 days
_fbc Analytics / Advertising Set by Meta when you arrive via a Facebook ad click. Only set if you accept cookies. 90 days

We do not use any other tracking cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, PostHog, or any other analytics platform at this time.

How to control cookies

When you first visit our site, a banner asks whether you accept or decline cookies. If you decline, no tracking cookies are set.

You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site, or by using your browser's cookie settings to block cookies from pipnavigator.co.uk and facebook.com.

Third-party cookies

The Meta Pixel may set cookies from the facebook.com domain. These are governed by Meta's privacy policy. We do not control those cookies but they are only loaded if you accept our cookie banner.
