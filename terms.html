What this site is

PIP Navigator (pipnavigator.co.uk) is a peer-support resource that helps PIP claimants in England and Wales describe their daily difficulties using wording aligned to PIP descriptors. It is not a legal service, benefits advice service, or medical evidence provider.

If you need independent, professional help with your claim, contact Citizens Advice (England: 0800 144 8848, Wales: 0800 702 2020).
